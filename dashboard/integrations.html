Step-by-Step Email Setup Guides

Option A: Resend (Recommended)

1

Go to resend.com and sign up for a free account (3k emails/month free).

2

Navigate to Domains → Add Domain, enter your custom domain (e.g. mycompany.com), and complete DNS verification.

3

Go to API Keys → Create API Key with "Sending Access" permissions and copy it.

4

Paste the key and sender email in the active tab above, then click Save Settings.

Option B: Gmail SMTP Integration

1

Go to your Google Account Settings and verify that 2-Step Verification is enabled under the Security tab.

2

Search for "App Passwords" in your account settings or visit Google App Passwords directly.

3

Enter an app name (e.g. Brightkey Portal) and click Create. Copy the unique 16-character password shown.

4

Paste your Gmail address and the 16-character App Password in the SMTP card above, then click Save Settings.
