Operational Status Flow

  • 1. Reserved (Now): Stock automatically deducted from `Available` and added to `Reserved`.
  • 2. Packed: Fulfillment team packages the lock and marks "Ready".
  • 3. Dispatched: Courier picks up the lock.
  • 4. Completed: Installer successfully mounts the lock, uploads proof, and completes job.
